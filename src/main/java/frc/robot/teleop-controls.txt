left trigger ...... intake
left bumper ....... outtake
X ................. autotarget
A ................. shoot distance override (set distance to low)
options ........... reset imu
B ................. deploy intake
Y ................. retract intake

hood will be down UNLESS feeding or autotargeting or shoot distance override (X or right trigger or A)
